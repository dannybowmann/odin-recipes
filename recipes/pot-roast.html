Pot Roast



In simple terms Pot Roast is a dish of meat, usually brisket of beef or chuck roast, stewed in one piece in a covered pot and served in its own gravy. However most people would describe it as the taste of childhood. The smell of this incredible dish wafting from moms kitchen was always able to make a day better. It a cost efficient meal that is a great skill to know how to cook, as it is delicious.

Ingredients you will need!

  • Shoulder cut chuck beef
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • flour
  • Red wine
  • Beef broth
  • Herbs
  • Yellow Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Tomato paste

Insructions!

  1. Heat the olive oil in a dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot over medium high heat. Add the seasoned beef and brown on all sides. Transfer to a plate.
  2. Add the onion and garlic to the same pot and saute until soft and translucent. Next, stir in the tomato paste and flour.
  3. Deglaze the pot with red wine before pouring in the beef broth, thyme, and bay leaves.
  4. Return the seared beef to the pot and surround it with carrots and potatoes. Cover with a lid and place in a hot oven.
  5. Let the pot roast cook for several hours or until the beef is very tender. Scoop into bowls, garnish with parsley or cilantro, and enjoy!
